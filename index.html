Who we are

A focused landman practice, built on doing the work right.

McCracken Land Services is an independent land services firm led by landman Michael McCracken. We handle the research, title, and field work that energy and real estate projects run on. This is detail work, the kind where one missed instrument or a bad chain of title costs real money. Every job is handled personally, so you get experienced attention from start to finish.

About

Meet the landman behind the work.

Michael McCracken has worked in the land business since 2004. He started McCracken Land Services so brokers and clients could work directly with an experienced landman instead of being handed down the line to whoever happens to be free.

His experience covers title examination and curative, mineral and surface ownership research, leasing and right of way, due diligence, courthouse and Corporation Commission research, and GIS mapping and deed plotting. If it touches land, title, or minerals, he has done it.

The approach is simple. Do the work right, and get it back when you said you would. Brokers and clients keep coming back because the details get handled and nothing slips through.
