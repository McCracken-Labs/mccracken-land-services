The format, in plain terms. Net Deed Plotter reads each straight course as Ndd.mmssE distance: quadrant, degrees, a period, then minutes and seconds as four digits, the second quadrant, a space, and the distance. So "North 22 degrees 13 minutes 10 seconds East, 200.7 feet" becomes N22.1310E 200.7. Varas keep a V on the distance so the plot scales right. Curves are not parsed yet, so enter any curve (direction, radius, arc length, central angle) by hand. Get the software at deedplotter.com. New to it?
